How should the league picker appear?

When the user taps a sport tab (e.g. Football), how do the league options show up?

A

Bottom Sheet

Tapping a sport slides up a half-screen sheet with leagues in a grid. Tap outside or the sport tab again to dismiss. Covers part of the content with a dimmed backdrop.

Floating Popover

Tapping a sport opens a compact floating panel just above the nav bar, no backdrop. Leagues in a grid. Tap outside to dismiss. Lightweight, non-blocking.

Inline Expansion

Tapping a sport expands the nav bar upward, showing leagues as a grid above the sport tabs — all within the nav bar. No overlay, no backdrop. Tapping again collapses.
